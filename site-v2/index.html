Questions families ask first

Clear answers before the review starts.

Do I need every heir ready before I ask for a review?

No. The review can start with what you know now, then identify which heir or probate questions still need attention.

Can HeirRight review taxes, liens, and title issues?

Yes. Those are core review areas. Some items may still need official documents, professional review, or family approval before a final decision.

Does a free review guarantee an offer?

No. The review is designed to clarify the situation first. If a sale makes sense, HeirRight can discuss the next step.
