Scope of Work

OPTION A  |  AI & AUTOMATION SYSTEMS

1. Automated Lead Report Engine

  • Source Adapters: Clerk of Courts, Property Appraiser, Probate Court, Tax Collector — plus integrated skip-trace enrichment.
  • Batch Download Architecture: County-wide record pulls by date range, not one-by-one manual queries. A single scheduled run downloads, parses, and normalizes hundreds of records in minutes.
  • Lead Capacity: Per automated run, the engine targets 200–400 raw probate and tax-delinquent records across Miami-Dade and Broward counties. After deduplication, enrichment, and AI scoring, this typically yields 80–150 qualified, contactable leads delivered directly to a CRM of your choice.
  • Proximity Search: Batch-download radius-based property clusters so you can target geographic zones efficiently, not random individual parcels.
  • Deduplication: Automatic matching by parcel ID and case number prevents double-work on the same estate or property.
  • Client owns all code and IP — no lock-in, no recurring platform fees.

2. CRM Migration & Pipeline Setup

  • Migrate from Google Spreadsheets to a CRM of your choice with full contact records, custom fields, and deal tracking.
  • Configure 3-phase pipeline: Marketing → Acquisition → Disposition.
  • Smart email sequences and SMS drip campaigns with personalized templates per heir relationship.

3. Document Preparation Automation

  • Offer letters, heir communication templates, and negotiation documents auto-populated from live lead data in the pipeline.
  • Pre-determined field mapping pulls owner name, property address, tax status, lien info, and heir contacts directly into document templates.
  • Output formats: print-ready PDF and editable Word documents with only minor manual adjustments required.
  • Claude AI integration for draft generation of status updates, follow-up letters, and counter-offer language.

4. Client-Facing Landing Page

  • One-page branded site matching the lead engine aesthetic — mobile-first, SEO-ready.
  • Integrated lead capture forms that flow directly into the CRM pipeline.

5. Integration & Workflow

  • Zapier automations connecting CRM, email, SMS, and AI drafting layers.
  • Scheduled tasks, lead scoring, and status triggers run without manual intervention.
  • 30-day post-implementation support window plus full technical documentation.
