# HeirRight Site Copy + Layout Drafts

Status: S10-T3 complete; Civic Ledger selected for S10-T4/S10-T5 completion
Last updated: 2026-06-03
Source posture: based on the S10 content intake, the S10 visual direction gallery, the HeirRight workflow packet, and current public-site positioning captured on June 3, 2026.

## Recommendation

Use **Civic Ledger** as the production direction unless the HeirRight team later asks to pivot.

Reason: it gives HeirRight the most room to look credible, estate-specific, and premium without sounding like a generic cash-buyer page. It also lines up best with the actual workflow: property review, deed/title checks, taxes, probate, heirs, and then a plain next step.

## Option A - Civic Ledger

Best for: a credible inherited-property review page that feels grounded, careful, and premium.

### Hero Copy

Headline:

> Untangle the estate. Keep the next step clear.

Subhead:

> HeirRight helps families review inherited property, title issues, taxes, liens, and heir questions before deciding whether to sell, hold, or resolve the property another way.

Primary CTA:

> Request a free property review

Secondary CTA:

> See how the review works

Trust line:

> Team-led review for South Florida families dealing with inherited property, taxes, title questions, and co-heir decisions.

### Homepage Layout

1. Hero
   Clear inherited-property headline, consultation CTA, phone number, and short trust line.
2. Where Families Get Stuck
   Four plain-language blocks: title questions, unpaid taxes, liens or recorded issues, missing heirs or probate.
3. The HeirRight Review
   Share the property, review the records, map the estate issues, outline the next options.
4. What We Can Help With
   Inherited houses, vacant properties, co-heir disagreements, back taxes, title friction, and properties that need a careful sale path.
5. Proof + Founder Trust
   HeirRight team story, approved testimonials, region served, and difficult-property examples.
6. Contact / Intake
   Address, name, phone, email, best time to reach you, and what is holding up the property.

### Trust Proof Placeholders

- Approved HeirRight company story.
- Approved testimonial quote.
- South Florida property-review experience.
- Attorney-fee support language, pending approved disclaimer.
- Difficult title/tax/heir problem examples, written without legal outcome guarantees.

## Option B - Warm Counsel

Best for: a more human, family-first page that lowers anxiety for sellers who are overwhelmed.

### Hero Copy

Headline:

> You do not have to sort through the estate alone.

Subhead:

> If an inherited home is tied up by paperwork, taxes, liens, probate, or family questions, HeirRight can help you understand what is attached to the property and what can happen next.

Primary CTA:

> Talk with HeirRight's team

Secondary CTA:

> What to expect

Trust line:

> Plain answers for families who need help understanding inherited-property options.

### Homepage Layout

1. Hero
   Warm headline, one consultation CTA, short testimonial pull quote, and direct phone path.
2. Start With What You Know
   Address, owner/estate name if known, family situation, and urgent property concerns.
3. What We Review
   Taxes, liens, title, probate, heirs, and whether a sale path is realistic.
4. A Calmer Way Forward
   Three-step process: share the property, get the review, choose the next move.
5. Stories + Proof
   Approved testimonials and HeirRight's company story.
6. Contact / Intake
   Friendly request form with a phone-first fallback.

### Trust Proof Placeholders

- Approved testimonial quote above the fold.
- Founder story as a trust-builder, not a long biography.
- "No pressure to sell before the situation is understood" positioning.
- Clear note that legal or tax advice comes from approved professionals only.

## Option C - Fast Relief

Best for: a direct-response page that keeps stronger urgency while staying honest about review gates.

### Hero Copy

Headline:

> Clear the inheritance bottleneck and see your options faster.

Subhead:

> When taxes, title problems, liens, co-heirs, or probate questions are holding up a property, HeirRight can review the situation and help you decide the next move.

Primary CTA:

> Start your property review

Secondary CTA:

> Call 786-962-3457

Trust line:

> Built for inherited homes where the sale path is not obvious yet.

### Homepage Layout

1. Hero
   Direct headline, phone CTA, property-review CTA, and three issue chips: taxes, title, heirs.
2. What Is Holding Up The Property?
   Fast scan of the common blockers HeirRight can review.
3. How The Review Works
   Submit the property, HeirRight checks the roadblocks, then the family reviews options.
4. When HeirRight Is A Fit
   Inherited homes, tax debt, liens, vacant properties, multiple heirs, and stuck paperwork.
5. Proof + Speed Without Hype
   Testimonials, process clarity, and timeline language that does not promise instant outcomes.
6. Contact / Intake
   Short form and click-to-call path.

### Trust Proof Placeholders

- Approved reviews tied to speed or problem solving.
- "Free review" language separated from any automatic offer claim.
- Clear qualifier around attorney-fee support.
- No countdowns, fake urgency, or guaranteed cash-offer language.

## Selection Notes

- Choose **Civic Ledger** if the priority is premium credibility and a better long-term brand.
- Choose **Warm Counsel** if the team wants the public site to feel more personal and pastoral.
- Choose **Fast Relief** if near-term conversion speed matters most, with careful guardrails against over-promising.
