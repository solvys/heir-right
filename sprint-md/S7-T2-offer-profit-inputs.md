# Sprint Brief: S7-T2 -- Offer/Profit Inputs

Owner: TP
Parent: S7-ORCH
Dependencies: S7-T1

## Goal

Represent offer/profit math inputs without final underwriting automation.

## Included Scope

- As-is value.
- Taxes due.
- Liens.
- Mortgages.
- Selling costs.
- Probate costs.
- Partition costs.
- Post-equity value.
- Number of heirs.
- Equity per heir.
- Buy percentage/value.
- Offer amount.
- Profit and minimum net profit.

## Excluded Scope

- Automatic offer approval.
- External offer send.

## Acceptance

- Offer math fields render in internal reports.
- Unknown values remain placeholders or review flags.
