# Sprint Brief: S5-T4 -- Lead Disqualification Queue

Owner: TP
Parent: S5-ORCH
Dependencies: S5-T1, S5-T2, S5-T3

## Goal

Surface blocked, disqualified, and review-needed leads as explicit operator states.

## Included Scope

- Company-owned state.
- Recently sold state.
- Missing property fact state.
- Missing title/deed fact state.
- Source blocked state.
- Manual review needed state.

## Excluded Scope

- Deleting or hiding disqualified leads by default.
- Final client override policy.

## Acceptance

- Dashboard/report can distinguish disqualified from blocked.
- Each state has a reason and next action.
- Operator can inspect why a lead stopped.
