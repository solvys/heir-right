# Sprint Brief: S6-T2 -- Probate/Civil/Family Docket Model

Owner: TP
Parent: S6-ORCH
Dependencies: S6-T1

## Goal

Model court docket research without converting records into legal advice.

## Included Scope

- Probate case number.
- Civil/family docket references.
- Case status field.
- Affidavit-of-heirs availability.
- Document request task shape.
- Official-record cross-link fields.

## Excluded Scope

- Legal conclusions about heirship or case outcome.
- Paid or authenticated court access.

## Acceptance

- Docket facts preserve source refs or review flags.
- Missing court documents become tasks.
