# Sprint Brief: S6-T4 -- Family Tree Hypothesis

Owner: TP
Parent: S6-ORCH
Dependencies: S6-T2, S6-T3

## Goal

Represent family-tree research as a hypothesis with evidence and unresolved questions.

## Included Scope

- Relationship nodes for spouse, children, parents, siblings, grandparents, aunts/uncles, cousins, nieces/nephews.
- Confidence/review status.
- Source refs per relationship.
- Contact placeholders.
- Unresolved question list.

## Excluded Scope

- Legal heir determination.
- Contact enrichment automation.

## Acceptance

- Lead report can show family tree hypothesis separately from confirmed facts.
- Every relationship has evidence or a review flag.
