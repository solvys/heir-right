# Sprint Brief: S6-T3 -- Marriage + Death Indicator Checks

Owner: TP
Parent: S6-ORCH
Dependencies: S6-T2

## Goal

Track marriage and death indicators as reviewable research facts.

## Included Scope

- Marriage-license signal field.
- DOB/DOD fields.
- Obituary links.
- Findagrave/Legacy/Google result placeholders.
- Death certificate status task.
- Incarceration status signal placeholder.

## Excluded Scope

- Paid VitalChek automation.
- Final spouse/heir determination.

## Acceptance

- Indicators can be included in lead reports with confidence/review state.
- Missing or ambiguous indicators stay flagged.
