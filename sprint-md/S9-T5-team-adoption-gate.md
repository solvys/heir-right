# Sprint Brief: S9-T5 -- Team Adoption Gate

Owner: TP
Parent: S9-ORCH
Dependencies: S9-T4

## Goal

Make team comfort and operator usability a required launch gate.

## Included Scope

- Operator workflow checklist.
- Sales rep queue usability.
- Lead handoff clarity.
- Non-technical edit path.
- Support/debug escalation.

## Excluded Scope

- Fast-tracking workflow changes without team validation.

## Acceptance

- CRM path cannot be marked production-ready until operator usability passes.
